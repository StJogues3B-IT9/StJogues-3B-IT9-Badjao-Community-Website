The Problem



Problem Statement


The lack of accessibility to clean water and food in the badjao community.


Problem Description

As long as it is not addressed, the Badjao community's lack of access to clean water and food will be a concern. In this instance, what we may refer to as a multiple issue with poverty is the fundamental reason for the Badjao community's lack of access to clean water and food. Both their financial situation and how they conduct their everyday duties are impacted. All of these problems taken together make it clearer that they have a very significant impact on their daily lives. I hope that this community will be able to raise the issue of access to clean water and food, especially for kids. They require our assistance if they are to be able to stay here on their own property, despite the fact that it is quite filthy. Around us, there is neither clean water nor food. I think individuals should support one another, but to make life better for the badjao community, we need a lot more people to step up. community, we need a lot more people to step up.

Project Summary

Unhygienic food and water access is a serious issue in the badjao community. These issues have been recognized as serious dangers to poor quality of life and human health. Due to the bad sanitation in the Badjao community, access to clean food and water has become a major problem. All of the people of the Badjao community experience health issues like diarrhea, starvation, and even death as a result of the unclean environment. Access to clean food and water are important because people can live well and have a decent quality of life if they have access to these. The benefit of having access to healthy food and water is that it will enhance their quality of life and create a happier, healthier community. By bringing communities together to help bring about change on issues, it raises and spreads awareness. People can feel encouraged and motivated to act or make changes when they are aware.

Project Background

The absence of clean food and water in the Badjao village is a problem that the community should take seriously. They are in need of decent food and water. Numerous things have changed as a result of the dry season, including health issues brought on by starvation and a lack of food. Discrimination are experienced by the badjao community association. They were labeled as having low emotional quotients, poor hygiene, and delayed learning. They struggle to engage with others and communicate their needs and wants due to a language barrier. The goal of the badjao community's advocacy work is to make everyone's living conditions better. Our goal is to increase awareness of and educate about local issues.

Proposed Project

Partner Institution Is Badjao Community. The Problem Of Partner Institutions is lack access to clean water and food. We can help the badjao community by spreading awareness, donating canned good foods and clean water. The Badjao Community requires immediate assistance from Filipinos as they consume unhealthy food and water daily. The badjao community should be provided with healthcare at all times so that it can boost their immune system. We intend to address the problem by using our group, and other people's talents and abilities in art and research to spread awareness to the badjao community by making posters and infographics. After making the posters and infographics, we will proceed to share it on social media as a way to educate the public on this issue.

Solution

The problem that the Badjao Community is facing is their issue in clean water and food. We plan to solve this by proposing the idea of home gardening, wherein they can grow fruits and vegetables in their own homes using natural fertilizers. This helps with their issue of lack of clean food since they are growing it themselves, no toxic or harmful pesticides are used. Growing plants also helps clean soil and water which can not only help with the Badjao Communitiy’s issue in clean water but also the environment as a whole. Using the Badjao Community’s compostables also helps reduce waste, recycles essential nutrients back into the soil, and creates a prolific soil ecosystem. This solution is better than other alternatives because it is sustainable and it “hits two birds with one stone”, helping both the Badjao Community and the environment. The materials that we would need for this proposal are, gardening materials (shovel, soil, pots, etc.), fruit and vegetable seedlings, natural compost (for fertilizer), and also the cooperation and teamwork of our group and the Badjao Community.
